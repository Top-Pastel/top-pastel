<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Tropical Modernism**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Vibrancy & Warmth**: Evoke the heat and energy of Brazil through color and light.
  2. **Organic Geometry**: Use soft curves and rounded shapes inspired by the pastel itself and natural forms.
  3. **Tactile Texture**: Incorporate subtle paper textures or flour-dusted effects to suggest the handmade nature of the product.
  4. **Direct & Appetizing**: Focus heavily on the product's visual appeal, making it look ready to eat.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: Deep Emerald Green (Brazil's forests) & Golden Yellow (Fried pastel crust).
  - **Secondary**: Warm White (Flour/Dough) & Terracotta (Earth/Clay).
  - **Intent**: To create a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels authentic and appetizing, stimulating hunger and nostalgia.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Split**: Hero section with a large, off-center product image bleeding off the edge.
  - **Floating Elements**: Ingredients or garnishes (parsley, tomato, cheese) floating subtly in the background parallax.
  - **Card-less Content**: Information flows directly on the background without heavy borders or containers, using whitespace for separation.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **"Crispy" Edges**: Buttons and key visual elements with slightly rough or organic edges, mimicking the fried dough.
  - **Golden Glow**: Subtle inner shadows and glows on product images to emphasize warmth.
  - **Patterned Backgrounds**: Faint, abstract patterns derived from Brazilian tile work (azulejos) in the footer or section breaks.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Hover to "Fry"**: Buttons turn from raw dough color to golden fried color on hover.
  - **Smooth Scroll**: Buttery smooth scrolling with elements fading in gently.

  **Animation**:
  - **Entrance**: Elements slide in with a soft bounce.
  - **Steam Effect**: Subtle, looping animation of steam rising from the hot pastel images.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Recorleta** or **Cooper Black** (Soft, rounded serif) - Friendly and nostalgic.
  - **Body**: **Nunito** or **Quicksand** (Rounded sans-serif) - Clean, modern, and approachable.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Neo-Brutalism / Pop Art**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Bold & Unapologetic**: High contrast, thick outlines, and loud typography.
  2. **Raw Authenticity**: showcasing the product "as is" without over-polishing, celebrating the street food culture.
  3. **Playful Chaos**: Elements slightly misaligned or overlapping to create energy.
  4. **High Impact**: Grabbing attention immediately with strong visuals.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: High-voltage Yellow & Stark Black.
  - **Secondary**: Bright Red (Ketchup/Hot Sauce) & Pure White.
  - **Intent**: To capture the vibrant, energetic, and sometimes chaotic spirit of a Brazilian street market (feira).

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Grid Breaker**: Rigid grids are ignored; elements are placed to maximize impact.
  - **Marquee Text**: Scrolling text banners announcing "FRESH", "CRISPY", "BRAZILIAN".
  - **Oversized Hero**: The product image takes up 80% of the viewport.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Thick Strokes**: All containers and buttons have heavy black borders (2px-4px).
  - **Drop Shadows**: Hard, non-blurred drop shadows at 45 degrees.
  - **Sticker Aesthetics**: "New", "Best Seller" badges that look like stickers.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Clicky Feedback**: Buttons depress visibly with a sharp movement.
  - **Cursor Effects**: Custom cursor that changes or has a trailing element.

  **Animation**:
  - **Snappy**: Instant transitions, no fades.
  - **Jiggle**: Elements vibrate slightly on hover.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Clash Display** or **Archivo Black** (Bold, industrial sans) - Loud and clear.
  - **Body**: **Space Mono** or **JetBrains Mono** (Monospace) - Utilitarian and raw.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Minimalist Luxury / "Artisan Bakery"**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Focus on Quality**: Highlighting the premium ingredients and the craft of the dough.
  2. **Serene & Clean**: A clutter-free environment that suggests purity and hygiene.
  3. **Elegant Typography**: Using type as a primary visual element.
  4. **Subtle Sophistication**: Elevating the humble pastel to a gourmet experience.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: Sage Green & Cream/Beige.
  - **Secondary**: Charcoal Grey (Text) & Muted Gold (Accents).
  - **Intent**: To convey a sense of premium quality, tradition, and artisanal care.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Central Axis**: Balanced, symmetrical layouts for a sense of stability and calm.
  - **Generous Margins**: Lots of breathing room around every element.
  - **Split Screen**: 50/50 split between high-res macro photography and elegant text.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Fine Lines**: 1px borders and dividers.
  - **Serif Elegance**: Use of italicized serif fonts for emphasis.
  - **Macro Photography**: Close-ups of the dough texture and bubbles.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Subtle Fades**: Everything transitions slowly and gracefully.
  - **Underline Hovers**: Links grow a fine line underline from center.

  **Animation**:
  - **Parallax**: Slow parallax on images.
  - **Fade Up**: Text fades up and in as you scroll.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Playfair Display** (High contrast serif) - Elegant and editorial.
  - **Body**: **Lato** or **Inter** (Clean sans-serif) - Legible and neutral.
</idea>
